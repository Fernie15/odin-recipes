Zucchini Bread

Description

If you're looking for something to bake that will make your home smell amazing and have your taste buds watering, look no further. This recipe for zucchini bread is amazing and will be even more amazing when you get to slice it up and eat it. Zucchini bread is a baked bread that is made of grated zucchini and incorporated into the batter. It's a pleasant way to enjoy a mild yet lightly sweet and cinnamony flavor, all baked into an amazing moist bread.

Zucchini bread can be enjoyed at any time of the day, typically it's served as a breakfast treat but it can also be enjoyed as a dessert. Many people like to use a spread whether it be butter, cream cheese, or just kept plain. This delicious baked bread will give you the flavor of the zucchinis mild taste and warm spices creating a comforting bread loved by many.

Ingredients

  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 1/2 cups of flour
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a medium size bowl mix the flour, cinnamon, baking soda, baking powder and salt.
  2. After the flour has been mixed with everything, peel and finely shred the zucchini.
  3. In another medium size bowl mix the sugar, the shredded zucchini, cooking oil, eggs, and vanilla.
  4. Combine both bowls into one large bowl and set it in an oven pan
  5. Pre-heat the oven to 325Â°F
  6. When oven is ready place the pan in the oven for an hour if using a metal pan. If using a glass pan bake for 1 hour and 20 minutes.
  7. Let the bread cool, slice, and enjoy!
